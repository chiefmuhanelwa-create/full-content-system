## Story Integration Rules

1. **20% rule** — Story time never exceeds 20% of total script length
2. **1-2 stories per script** — More dilutes the teaching content
3. **Proof not plot** — The story proves a point, not tells a tale for its own sake
4. **Numbers are mandatory** — Vague stories are weak. Specific stories are weapons.
5. **Placement** — Stories land AFTER the teaching point, as evidence. Not as the opener.
6. **Emotional arc always** — Every story must have Before → During → After → Lesson
7. **Never boastful** — Lead with the struggle. The win comes last.

### Story Script Template
```
[TEACHING POINT — 60-80% of time]

"I learned this the hard way. [STORY SNIPPET — 20% max]"

[LESSON — the connection between story and teaching]

[CTA — what to do with this information now]
```
